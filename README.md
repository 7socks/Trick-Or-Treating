# Stardew Valley Trick-Or-Treating Mod
It's the last day of fall, All Hallow's Eve in Stardew Valley... Pelican Town is celebrating with an annual trick-or-treating festival. Everyone is dressed up in costume, special treats are being given out on doorsteps, and the town is full of spooky decorations and glowing lanterns.

## Features
- Trick-Or-Treating Festival held on Fall 28
- Costume sprites and portraits for most villager NPCs
- NPCs wear their costumes to the Spirits Eve festival and all day for Trick-Or-Treating
- Pierre's candy stand open during festival hours (4PM-10PM)
- Several candy items exclusive to the Trick-Or-Treating event
- Cooking recipe for Candy Apple

## Dependencies
- SMAPI
- Content Patcher

## Mod Compatibility
This mod may be incompatible with other mods, especially those which add new NPCs or locations. This should only affect gameplay on the modded festival days (Fall 27 & 28).

Due to how the game handles alternate maps for passive festival days, map edits from other mods will not be applied to this mod's festival maps on Fall 28. This includes warp tiles, which can make additional modded locations inaccessible. The affected locations are the Forest, Town, Beach, and Mountain.
